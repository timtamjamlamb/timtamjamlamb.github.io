Titanic Competition

click to goto competition

After entering the last competition I felt joining another HERE. This was a dataset on the survival of passengers on the Titanic. This was a classification investigation and that is something I have little experience in Machine Learning.

There was more data visualisation whilst comparing the factors and their survival.

The model building was a more simple than the house regression and I ended up with a prediction that was very weak in terms of guessing the survival of the passengers.

I believe this was due to the features used being wrong, not scaling my dataset and also the data pre-processing being done incorrectly. So in the future I would like to reinvestigate this and fix these errors to predict the survival better.
